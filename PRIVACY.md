# FaceColour — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-19_

FaceColour is designed to be private by default. This policy explains what the app
does with your data.

## Short version
- Your photos are analyzed **on your device**. They are **not uploaded** to us or anyone else.
- Your saved analyses and thumbnails are stored **only on your device**.
- We have **no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no third‑party advertising SDKs**.

## Camera and photos
FaceColour uses your camera or photo library only to let you provide a selfie for
color analysis. Face detection and skin‑tone analysis run locally on your device.
Images you select are not transmitted off the device by the app.

## Saved history
If you tap **Save**, the app stores the analysis result and a small thumbnail in the
app's private storage on your device. You can delete any saved item at any time, which
also deletes its thumbnail. Uninstalling the app removes all saved data.

## Shopping ("Shop your colors")
In the default build, product suggestions come from sample data bundled in the app and
no network request is made.

If a remote product provider is configured by the developer, the app may send only the
**season and shade tone** of your result (e.g. "autumn", "tone 6") to that provider to
retrieve matching products. It does **not** send your photo or any personal identifiers.
Tapping a product opens the retailer's website in your browser; that site is governed by
the retailer's own privacy policy.

## Data we collect
None off‑device. We do not collect, sell, or share personal information.

## Children
FaceColour does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date
